2. **0:15-0:30 Thesis** - Four product pillars assemble as a data pipeline.
3. **0:30-0:45 Live Intelligence** - Lab screenshot pushes forward; 377 news, 1277 nodes, 2761 relationships count up.
4. **0:45-1:00 Send And Generate** - Submit action moment: process stream types on beside the map.
5. **1:00-1:15 Graph Effects** - 2D/3D graph screenshots split and pulse, showing special effects clearly.
6. **1:15-1:30 Synthetic Data Engine** - Model hero screen with OHLCV transformation pipeline.
7. **1:30-1:45 Market Proof** - Market cards highlight positive improvements across four markets.
8. **1:45-2:00 Strategy Test** - Strategy initial/loading screens show the run button and calculation state.
9. **2:00-2:15 Strategy Result** - 国盾量子 metric cards compare cumulative return.
10. **2:15-2:30 Risk And Sharpe** - Same result screenshot, now highlighting Sharpe and drawdown improvement.

12. **2:45-3:00 Close** - Product proof ladder: graph, model, backtest, single stock, then CTA.

## Transition Direction

Use a consistent blur-through transition between evidence scenes, with two stronger zoom-through moments: entering the synthetic data engine and entering the strategy result. Each scene animates in; previous scenes are covered by the incoming scene rather than animated out.
